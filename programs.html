Critical Industrial Technologies Programs Overview

The Critical Industrial Technologies (CIT) initiative offers a collection of 5 programs to drive Critical Technology adoption within key sectors, for maximum impact across Ontario industries.


Programs are targeted to the specific needs of Ontario Small-and-Medium-Enterprises (SMEs).

Please check the program documents here

Minimum Eligibility Criteria

The Critical Industrial Technology programs are a good fit if your company meets these minimum criteria :


  • 1. Small/Medium Enterprise (less than 500 employees)

  • 2. For-profit organization

  • 3. Registered operations in Ontario

  • 4. Adoption of Critical Technologies part of your roadmap

The 5 programs are designed to support SMEs where they are at, to strategically prepare businesses for the future.

Explore the CIT programs below and select the program that you want to learn more about :



Development and Commercialization Program (DC):

Eligible SMEs gain free access to a Technology Development Site to develop and showcase their Critical Technology solutions in an operational environment that mimics the desired market, with $50-200k of support from OCI.

Ideal for Accelerated Product Development : SMEs seeking to accelerate, de-risk, and offset the cost of their Critical Technology product development.



Technnology Access Program(TAP):

Eligible SMEs can access a Technology Development Site at no cost to validate their Critical Technology product and hit the market faster.

Ideal for Product Validation : SMEs with an existing Critical Technology product or service that would benefit from testing in an operational environment that mimics their target market.



Sector Adoption Program(SA):

The Sector Adoption Program presents an opportunity, via CIT challenge statement, for a consortium of partners to collaborate on a solution that catalyzes impactful change for an entire sector. Up to $1 million available for a multi-partner consortium with a minimum of 1 SME.

Ideal for Sector Innovation : Industry leaders seeking to collaborate on province-wide innovations that address Critical Technology challenges across four key sectors.



Talent Development Internship Program(TDI):

Eligible SMEs can invest in Critical Technology internships to bring on new, young talent to create the industrial workforce of tomorrow.

Ideal for New Talent : Growing SMEs invested in modernizing their workforce to thrive in the dynamic future of Critical Technologies.



Future Ready Program(FR):

Eligible SMEs can invest in existing employees to upskill/reskill staff for the challenges of today.

Ideal for Talent Development and Re/Upskilling : SMEs looking for growth opportunities for their staff to maintain market competitiveness.
