Development and Commercialization Program !

Applications open for rolling intake, up to $200k available.

Eligible Ontario Small-and-Medium Enterprises (less than 500 staff) can receive $50k - $200k to derisk and offset the costs of their product development activities, including free access to a Technology Development Site.

SME Benefits

  • 1. Cutting-edge product development

  • 2. Increased momentum for market penetration

  • 3. Ecosystem navigation support

  • 4. Free TDS access to validate products

  • 5. Accelerated product development

  • 6. Strategic innovation partners

  • 7. Network development 

Program Details

The Development and Commercialization Program provides companies a unique opportunity to accelerate the launch of their Critical Technology solutions with up to $200k of OCI support and TDS access.


Technology Development Sites (TDS) unlock an operational environment that reflects target markets for SMEs to validate their solutions, accelerating their go-to-market timeline within CIT core sectors.


Companies may choose to collaborate with a strategic partner that represents significant potential commercialization opportunities.


Critical Technologies include: 5G and Advanced Networks, AI, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Quantum and Robotics.

Eligibility

Open to Ontario-based for-profit Small-and-Medium-Enterprises (less than 500 staff).

SME solution must incorporate at least one of the named Critical Technologies.

Required usage of at least 1 TDS for a minimum of 2 hours.

Six months maximum of TDS access.  

Funding to be matched 1:1 by applicant company.

Optional strategic partner.

Explore the growing roster of TDS sites here.

Applicant Profile


This program is suitable for companies with Critical Technology products (fully realized or in-development) that are looking to accelerate commercialization within the core CIT sectors: Advanced Manufacturing (non-auto), Agri-Food, Construction, and Mining.

The first step is to contact an OCI Business Development Manager (BD) to determine project eligibility and program compatibility. See the Business Development Directory here.
